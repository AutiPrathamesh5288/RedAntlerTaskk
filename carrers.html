Red Antler

All of our work is about reimagining what’s possible, whether that’s creating a new idea from scratch or completely reinventing a category. Our team is motivated by diving into the unknown, asking hard questions, and finding creative solutions to problems that have never before been solved. 

 

Most people here don’t fit perfectly into a job description, but our areas of focus include Strategy, Research, Brand Identity, UX/UI Design, Copywriting, Marketing & Advertising, Production, Industrial Design, and Engineering.

 

We’re a hybrid team who values flexibility and choice, so there’s no mandate to be in the office on X number of days a week. We get together in-person when we believe it will make the work and our relationships stronger.

We’re working to change the industry

 

When we think about shaping the future, we also look inward. Diversity, equity, and inclusion are a constant work in progress and we’re committed to doing the work that needs to be done. We are focused on education, training, recruitment, and mentorship to create a more inclusive and equitable world.
