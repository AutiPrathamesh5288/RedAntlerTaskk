Red Antler

Every business we work with is the best at what it does. Cookies that’ll make you cry (good tears). A corporate card that actually helps you spend less. The flying cars you dreamed of as a child. Robot butlers (more or less).

 

But being the best is no longer enough. In every category there are endless options, and if that’s not true yet, it will be. It’s only a matter of time before someone shows up in a snazzy suit and tries to steal your crown. Our job is to make their job impossible.

 

There are many definitions of brand. Countless books on the topic—we even wrote one. But it all boils down to one simple thing: why should people care? We create brand experiences that get people to notice and care. We turn transactions into love stories (minus the part in the middle where the two protagonists are separated by forces out of their control).

-Red Antler
